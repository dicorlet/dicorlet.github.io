About Me

I'm a GIS specialist with a background in environmental science and watershed hydrology. My work focuses on translating complex spatial datasets into clear, actionable maps and analyses that support land management and conservation decisions.

I work primarily in QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, and GRASS GIS, with Python for scripting and automation. I build interactive web maps with Leaflet.js to make spatial data accessible to broader audiences.
